# Companies Working in Quantum Technology

The quantum industry consists of a mix of large technology companies, specialized quantum startups, government-linked organizations, and deep-tech firms. Understanding who is doing what helps students target internships, jobs, and collaborations effectively.

This section provides a **structured overview of major quantum companies and their focus areas**.

---

## Types of quantum companies

Quantum companies broadly fall into four categories:
1. Large technology corporations
2. Quantum-first startups
3. Hardware-focused deep-tech firms
4. Application and software-focused companies

Each category offers different roles and expectations.

---
