# Quantum Research Labs in India

India has a rapidly growing quantum research ecosystem spanning universities, national laboratories, and industry-linked research centers. For students, early exposure to these labs through internships or projects is one of the most effective ways to build a quantum career.

This section lists **key Indian institutions and how students can engage with them**.

---

## How to approach Indian labs

Recommended approach:
1. Identify specific research groups
2. Read recent papers or projects
3. Prepare a focused email
4. Attach a concise CV and GitHub
5. Clearly state availability and goals

Generic emails rarely work.

## What labs look for

Labs typically value:
- Strong fundamentals
- Demonstrated interest
- Prior projects
- Ability to learn independently
- Clear communication

Grades alone are not sufficient.

## Final note

Indian quantum labs offer high-quality research exposure.

Proactive outreach, preparation, and consistency matter far more than institutional labels.
